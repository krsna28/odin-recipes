CHICKEN BIRYANI

Chicken biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent.
It is a popular one-pot meal that combines fragrant basmati rice with succulent chicken pieces, aromatic spices, and herbs.
The dish is known for its rich flavors, tender meat, and vibrant colors, making it a favorite among biryani lovers worldwide.

INGREDIENTS
Chicken Marinade Rice Biryani
  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp ghee or vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro leaves
  • Saffron strands soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
  • Fried onions for garnish (optional)

LETS MAKE BIRYANI



  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices.
  3. Saut√© onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste in ghee or oil.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Layer partially cooked rice over chicken and add mint and cilantro.
  6. Drizzle saffron-infused milk and garnish with fried onions.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked and flavors meld together.
