CHICKEN

Chicken is a versatile and widely loved meat that can be prepared in countless delicious ways.
Whether you prefer grilled, roasted, fried, or stewed chicken, there are endless possibilities to explore.
Tender and flavorful, chicken dishes are enjoyed in various cuisines around the world, offering a wide range of tastes and textures to satisfy different palates.

INGREDIENTS



  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Herbs and spices of your choice (e.g., garlic powder, paprika, thyme, rosemary)

LETS MAKE CHICKEN



  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and your desired herbs and spices. You can use a combination of garlic powder, paprika, thyme, and rosemary for added flavor.
  3. Heat the olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they develop a golden brown crust.
